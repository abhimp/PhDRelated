\Bio{
Abhijit Mondal did his my Bachelore of Technology (B Tech) from Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University Of Technology, West Bengal (former West Bengal University Of Technology) in 2010 and Master in Technology (M Tech) from Indian Institue of Technology Guwahati in 2012. He has worked in Rovi (now Tivo) as a Software Developer for 2.5 Years (2012 - 2015). His research interests are online video streaming and computer networks.
